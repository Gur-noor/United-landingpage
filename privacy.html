Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 4, 2026

No Sale of Personal Information

We collect information you provide when you submit an offering-materials or contact request. We use that information to respond, review inquiries, operate the site, and support compliance and security. We do not sell personal information.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use this Site. It does not apply to third-party sites or services that may be linked or integrated, such as verification providers, payment providers, or external platforms.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Contact details such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Inquiry details such as jurisdiction, investor category, indicative allocation, acknowledgments, notes, and any identifiers you provide.
  • Communications you send to us through email or forms.

2.2 Automatically collected information

When you use the Site, basic technical information may be logged by standard web infrastructure, such as IP address, device or browser type, referring pages, timestamps, and pages viewed. This information helps with security, operations, analytics, and performance.

3. How We Use Information

  • To respond to requests and communicate with you.
  • To review private-offering inquiries and support eligibility, compliance, and verification workflows.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

4. Sharing and Disclosures

We do not sell personal information. We may share information in the following situations:

  • Service providers: With vendors that help operate the Site, such as hosting, security, analytics, communications, compliance, and verification tools, subject to appropriate obligations.
  • Legal and safety: If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security of the project, users, and the public.
  • Business operations: In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets, subject to applicable law.

5. Cookies and Analytics

The Site may use cookies or similar technologies to support basic functionality and analytics. You can often control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features.

6. Payments and Third Parties

If a future transaction is processed through a third-party payment, escrow, verification, or subscription provider, that third party may process your information under its own privacy policy. We do not receive full payment card or bank details from those providers unless they separately provide them to us through an authorized reporting process.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law or necessary for legitimate business purposes such as security, compliance, or dispute resolution.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices and Rights

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting us at the email below.
  • You may opt out of non-essential communications by requesting removal.
  • Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights regarding your information.

10. Children

The Site is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
